Server rules

The official home of The Bob SMP

Core rules

  • Respect all players and staff. No harassment, hate speech, or targeted toxicity.
  • No hacking, cheating, x-ray, duping, or use of unfair client modifications.
  • No griefing or stealing from other players unless clearly allowed by a server event or rule set.
  • Keep chat reasonably clean. Light swearing is fine; slurs and personal attacks are not.
  • Follow staff instructions at all times. If you disagree, open a ticket instead of arguing in chat.

Building & claims

  • Do not build too close to other players without their permission.
  • No lag machines, massive redstone clocks, or intentional TPS killers.
  • Large farms are allowed, but must be reasonably optimized and not left running unattended.
  • Do not destroy terrain around spawn or community areas without approval.

PvP & conflict

  • Unagreed PvP is only allowed if clearly stated in the server rules or region.
  • Organised wars or events must be approved by staff.
  • Targeted harassment, spawn-killing, or repeated griefing is not allowed.

Chat & Discord behaviour

  • No advertising other servers without explicit staff permission.
  • No doxxing, sharing personal information, or posting NSFW content.
  • Use the correct channels in Discord for support, reports, and suggestions.

Enforcement

Staff may issue warnings, mutes, kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on the severity and frequency of rule breaks. Attempting to bypass punishments (alts, VPNs, etc.) may result in permanent removal from the community.
